# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

German recruiters and hiring managers reviewing candidates for working-student and internship roles in data and business intelligence, supply-chain analytics, and applied AI. They need to understand Vittorio Cai's positioning, evidence, availability, and contact path within about one minute.

## Product Purpose

Present Vittorio as a cross-functional candidate who connects business understanding, data analysis, machine learning, and useful software. Success means a recruiter can quickly identify the strongest projects, verify Vittorio's specific contribution, switch language without losing context, and reach a public project, CV, or contact link without friction.

## Brand Personality

Precise, useful, quietly confident. The site should feel like a carefully edited technology and business publication: direct, structured, credible, and restrained without becoming anonymous.

## Anti-references

- Generic developer portfolios with gradient heroes, floating glass cards, skill meters, fake dashboards, or decorative metrics.
- CV-shaped pages that list everything without establishing a clear professional position.
- Literal imitation of NetEase branding, logos, or proprietary visual assets.
- Overdesigned magazine styling, novelty fonts, excessive animation, and repeated ornamental section labels.
- Claims or visuals that overstate team-project ownership or invent performance evidence.

## Design Principles

- Make the evidence scannable before making it decorative.
- Use a strict editorial grid and disciplined whitespace to signal clarity.
- Give the strongest verified work the most visual weight.
- Keep every public claim, action, and project boundary defensible.
- Preserve equal quality across English, German, and Simplified Chinese.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, reduced motion, 200% text enlargement, 320 CSS-pixel reflow, adequate small-text contrast, and 44 CSS-pixel mobile touch targets where practical. Language metadata and route-preserving language alternatives must remain complete.
